Pierogi

Description

Pierogi is a traditional polish dish, made of dough and filled with cheese, potatoes, fruit or many more. It is served with heavy cream, crumbles or with some grease.

Pierogi are made all over the country and there are many methods of doing it and fillings according to the particular regions.

Ingredients

  • 1000 g of flour
  • 80 g of butter
  • a pinch of salt
  • 400 ml of boiling hot water
  • 2 large onions
  • 1000 g of potatoes
  • 500 g of cottage cheese
  • salt,pepper

Steps

  1. Mix flour with salt and add cold, chopped butter.
  2. Add hot water step by step and chop a dough with a knife until all ingredients are harshly combined.
  3. Knead the dough with hands for 10 minutes until it's elastic and nice.
  4. Chop and fry the onion on an oil of choice.
  5. Boil potatoes and mash them, then mix with cottage cheese and onion. Add salt and pepper for taste.
  6. Create small rings of dough and fill them with a little bit of filling. Create a shape of the edges with fingers.
  7. Cook pierogi in hot water in portions for 5 to 7 minutes each.
