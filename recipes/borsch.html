Borsch

Description

Borsch is a soup made of pickled flour and as strange as it sounds, it is really delicious dish, often made and eaten during Easter in Poland. It is served with sausage and eggs, also with potatoes and bread too.

Ingredients

  • 300 g of sausage
  • 200 g of smoked bacon
  • 1 carrot, 1 celery, 1 parsley, 1 onion
  • 100 g heavy cream
  • 500 g of sourdough made from wheat flour
  • herbs
  • salt and pepper
  • 6 eggs

Steps

  1. To the big pot add sausage, bacon, vegetables, herbs and cook for 30 minutes.
  2. Add sourdugh and mix.
  3. Add heavy cream then add salt and pepper for taste
  4. Boil eggs in a small pot.
  5. Serve on a deep plate with boiled eggs, fresh bread and sausage.
