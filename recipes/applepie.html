Apple pie

Description

Apple pie, in polish named 'szarlotka', is a traditional polish pie with a special, sour kind of apples and some shortbread on the bottom with crumble on top.

Ingredients

  • 1500 g of apples
  • 160 g of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 300 g of flour
  • 250 g of butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Skin the apples, chop them into smaller pieces and put to the large pan.
  2. Add 80 g sugar and cinnamon and fry for about 20 minutes, mixing once in a while until soft.
  3. Mix flour with cold chopped butter, add 80 g sugar mix together. Add 1 egg and mix again.
  4. Divide the dough in half and put it to the freezer for 15 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180 Celcius. Put half of the dough in the baking tray, lay apples and then put the second part of the dough on top.
  6. Bake for about 50 minutes until golden.
